# InstaInstru Color Usage Guide

## Color Palette

- **Bold Purple** — `#6A0DAD`
- **Bold Yellow** — `#FFC300`
- **Confident Purple** — `#D4B5F0`
- **Confident Yellow** — `#FFE580`
- **White** — `#FFFFFF`
- **Gray** — `#F8F8F8`
- **Dark Gray** — `#333333`

---

## Color Applications

### Bold Purple (#6A0DAD)
**Used for primary actions and brand identity**
- Main logo text "iNSTAiNSTRU"
- Primary action buttons ("Search", "Book Now", "Sign Up")
- Section headings ("How it works", "Available right now", "Trending in your area")
- Active navigation states and menu items
- Text links throughout the site
- Selected/active category pills
- "Sign up / Log in" text and border

### Bold Yellow (#FFC300)
**Reserved for special moments and urgency**
- Special CTAs only ("Available in 2 hours!", "Last spot today")
- Star ratings (★★★★★)
- Success checkmarks and completion badges
- Limited-time promotions or sales
- New feature announcement banners
- Trending percentage indicators (↑12%)
- "Book Now" on featured instructors only

### Confident Purple (#D4B5F0)
**Used for ambient backgrounds and gentle interactions**
- Category pills (default state) - Language, Music, Fitness, etc.
- Alternating section backgrounds
- Card hover state backgrounds
- "How it works" cards background
- App promotion section background
- Footer background
- Instructor card hover effects

### Confident Yellow (#FFE580)
**Used sparingly for highlighting special content**
- Featured/promoted instructor card backgrounds
- Notification bars at top of page
- Special offer banner backgrounds
- Success message backgrounds
- "New instructor" badges
- Yellow accents in illustrations/graphics only
- Hover state for special promotions

### White (#FFFFFF)
**Primary content background**
- Main page background
- Instructor cards (default state)
- Form inputs and search bars
- Main content areas
- Card backgrounds
- Modal backgrounds
- Navigation bar background

### Gray (#F8F8F8)
**Subtle separation and secondary areas**
- Alternate section backgrounds (every other section)
- Input field backgrounds
- Disabled button states
- Horizontal divider lines
- Secondary information boxes
- Loading state backgrounds
- Dropdown menu backgrounds

### Dark Gray (#333333)
**All text content**
- All body text and paragraphs
- Secondary headings
- Descriptions under titles
- Form labels and placeholders
- Card descriptions
- Time and date text
- Price information

---

## Section-by-Section Guide

### Hero Section
- Background: White
- "Instant learning with": Dark Gray
- "iNSTAiNSTRU": Bold Purple
- Search button: Bold Purple
- Category pills: Confident Purple background, Bold Purple text (on hover)

### How It Works
- Background: White
- Section title: Bold Purple
- Cards: Confident Purple background
- Card titles: Bold Purple
- Card descriptions: Dark Gray

### Available Right Now
- Background: Gray
- Section title: Bold Purple
- Instructor cards: White
- "Book Now" buttons: Bold Purple (Bold Yellow only if last spot)
- Instructor names: Dark Gray

### Trending in Your Area
- Background: White
- Section title: Bold Purple
- Service icons background: White with Gray border
- Percentage increases: Bold Yellow
- Service names: Dark Gray

### App Promotion
- Background: Confident Purple
- Tagline: Bold Purple
- App store buttons: Original (black)

### Footer
- Background: Confident Purple
- Links: Dark Gray (Bold Purple on hover)

---

## Special Rules

1. **Never use Bold Yellow for regular buttons** - Reserve for urgency only
2. **Don't mix Confident Yellow and Confident Purple** in the same section
3. **White space is your friend** - Don't fill every section with color
4. **Test text contrast** - Ensure Dark Gray is readable on all backgrounds
5. **Mobile considerations** - Bold colors should be slightly smaller on mobile to avoid overwhelming

---

## Quick Reference

**High Impact**: Bold Purple + White
**Special Moments**: Bold Yellow (sparingly)
**Gentle Branding**: Confident Purple
**Rare Highlights**: Confident Yellow
**Clean Base**: White + Gray
