## InstaInstru Brand Colors Applied

## Visual Design Specifications

### InstaInstru Brand Colors Applied
- **Modal Background**: #FFFFFF (White)
- **Category bars**: #D4B5F0 (Confident Purple)
- **Category text**: #333333 (Dark Gray)
- **Skill cards**: #FFFFFF (White) with #D4B5F0 border
- **Skill card hover**: #D4B5F0 (Confident Purple) background
- **Selected skill checkmark**: #FFC300 (Bold Yellow)
- **Main heading**: #6A0DAD (Bold Purple)
- **Body text/descriptions**: #333333 (Dark Gray)
- **Primary button**: #6A0DAD (Bold Purple) background, white text
- **Submit request button**: #6A0DAD (Bold Purple) text, white background
- **Price input backgrounds**: #F8F8F8 (Gray)
- **Selected skills section**: #F8F8F8 (Gray) background
- **Success messages**: #FFC300 (Bold Yellow) checkmark
- **Error text**: #6A0DAD (Bold Purple)
- **"Don't see your skill?" section**: #D4B5F0 (Confident Purple) background

### Typography
- **Main title**: 24px, bold, #6A0DAD (Bold Purple)
- **Subtitle**: 14px, regular, #333333 (Dark Gray)
- **Category names**: 16px, medium, #333333 (Dark Gray)
- **Skill names**: 14px, regular, #333333 (Dark Gray)
- **Price inputs**: 20px, bold, #333333 (Dark Gray)
- **Button text**: 16px, medium, white on purple background
- **Remove links**: 14px, regular, #6A0DAD (Bold Purple)

### Spacing
- **Modal padding**: 32px
- **Category spacing**: 8px between
- **Skill card margin**: 8px
- **Selected skills padding**: 16px
- **Mobile padding**: 16px

### Animations
- **Category expand**: 300ms ease-out
- **Skill selection**: 200ms scale + fade
- **Price validation**: Instant feedback
- **Success messages**: Fade in/out 300ms
