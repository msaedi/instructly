# InstaInstru Success Metrics

## North Star Metric
**Monthly Active Bookings (MAB)** - Unique students who complete at least one booking per month

Target: 10,000 MAB by Month 6

## Primary KPIs

### 1. Acquisition Metrics
- **Search → Results**: >90% find relevant instructors
- **First-Time Booking Rate**: >25% of new visitors book within 7 days
- **App Download → First Booking**: <3 days average
- **Organic vs Paid Split**: Target 60/40 by Month 6

### 2. Activation Metrics
- **Sign-up → First Search**: >80% within first session
- **View Instructor → Contact/Book**: >15% conversion
- **Availability View → Booking Start**: >30% conversion
- **Time to First Booking**: <5 minutes for returning users

### 3. Engagement Metrics
- **Bookings per Active Student**: >2.5 per month
- **Repeat Booking Rate**: >60% book same instructor again
- **Cross-Category Booking**: >20% try multiple skills
- **Session Frequency**: Weekly users >40%

### 4. Retention Metrics
- **Month 1 Retention**: >70% make 2nd booking
- **Month 3 Retention**: >50% still active
- **Month 6 Retention**: >35% still active
- **Reactivation Rate**: >25% return after 30-day absence

### 5. Revenue Metrics
- **Average Booking Value**: $85
- **Commission Rate**: 20% platform fee
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: >3:1 by Month 6
- **Payment Failure Rate**: <2%

## Operational Excellence

### Search & Discovery
- **Search Success Rate**: >85% find what they need
- **"No Results" Rate**: <5% of searches
- **Filter Usage**: >40% apply at least one filter
- **Search → Booking**: <4 clicks average

### Booking Flow
- **Flow Completion Rate**: >70% who start, finish
- **Abandonment Points**: <20% drop at payment
- **Error Rate**: <1% technical failures
- **Booking Modification Rate**: <15% change/cancel

### Trust & Safety
- **Instructor Verification Rate**: 100% background checked
- **Safety Incident Rate**: <0.01% of bookings
- **Review Submission Rate**: >60% leave reviews
- **Trust Score**: >4.5/5 platform average

### Mobile Performance
- **App Load Time**: <2 seconds
- **Time to Interactive**: <3 seconds
- **Crash Rate**: <0.5%
- **API Response Time**: <200ms p95

## Experience Quality Metrics

### Student Satisfaction
- **NPS Score**: >50 (Excellent)
- **App Store Rating**: >4.5 stars
- **Support Ticket Rate**: <5% of bookings
- **Resolution Time**: <2 hours average

### Instructor Success
- **Instructor Fill Rate**: >70% of available slots
- **Instructor Retention**: >80% active after 6 months
- **Earnings per Instructor**: >$2,000/month average
- **Instructor NPS**: >60

### Platform Health
- **Match Rate**: >95% successful pairings
- **Rebooking Intent**: >80% plan to book again
- **Referral Rate**: >30% refer a friend
- **Multi-Session Packages**: >25% buy bundles

## Leading Indicators (Weekly Tracking)

1. **New Student Signups**: 500+ per week
2. **Instructor Applications**: 50+ per week
3. **Search Volume**: 10,000+ searches/week
4. **Availability Posted**: 1,000+ hours/week
5. **Support Contact Rate**: <3% of users

## Cohort Analysis

Track weekly cohorts for:
- Booking velocity (time to 1st, 2nd, 3rd booking)
- Retention curves
- Revenue per cohort
- Feature adoption rates

## A/B Testing Metrics

For each test, measure impact on:
- Conversion rate (primary)
- Booking completion time
- User satisfaction score
- Revenue per user
- Retention impact
