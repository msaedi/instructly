## ğŸ¯ Conversion Optimization Tips

### Reduce Friction
1. **Single Page Checkout** - Don't split across pages
2. **Auto-advance** - Between fields on mobile
3. **Smart Defaults** - Save card by default
4. **Clear CTAs** - "Pay $96.00" not just "Submit"

### Build Confidence
1. **Show instructor photo** during payment
2. **Cancellation policy** visible but not scary
3. **Success stories** or rating count
4. **Response time** - "Usually responds in 2 hours"

### Quick Wins
- Autofocus first field
- Disable submit until valid
- Real-time validation
- Clear error placement
- Progress indicator
