  display: flex;
  flex-direction: column;

  transition: color 0.2s ease;

## Interaction Pattern:

1. **On Page Load**: Fitness is pre-selected (middle category)
2. **Click Category**:
   - Previous selection fades
   - New category gets black text + yellow underline
   - Subcategories slide in below
3. **Hover Effects**:
   - Categories: Darker grey
   - Pills: Yellow border
4. **Mobile**: Horizontal scroll with touch-friendly spacing

This design maintains TaskRabbit's minimal aesthetic while incorporating InstaInstru's yellow accent color for selection states.
